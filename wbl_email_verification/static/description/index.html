Work Flow of Email Verification :

Detailed Features and Steps:

  • Mandatory Email Verification: New users must verify their email addresses to access their accounts, enhancing security and reducing fraudulent sign-ups. .
  • Verification Notification Email: Upon signing up, an automated verification email is sent to the user, ensuring timely confirmation of their account.
  • Customizable User Restrictions: Admins can choose to hide prices and the "Add to Cart" button for unverified users, preventing them from interacting with product offerings until verification is complete.
  • Persistent Alert Slider: An alert slider is displayed prominently under the header, prompting users to verify their email addresses. Admins can customize the text, colors, and background of this alert to match the website theme.
  • Access Control for Unverified Users:Admins have the ability to restrict access to the shop and website for users who have not completed email verification, further securing the e-commerce platform.
Benefits of Email Verification:

Increased Security

  • Email verification minimizes the risk of spam accounts and ensures that only legitimate users access the platform.

Enhanced User Engagement

  • The verification process encourages users to complete their registration, fostering engagement from the start.

Improved Control for Admins

  • Customize user experiences and restrict functionalities to promote email verification effectively.

Professional Appearance

  • A well-defined verification process enhances the credibility of your e-commerce website.
