Download Catalog PDF

The Download Catalog PDF app for Odoo allows businesses to generate and provide downloadable PDF catalogues of their products or services directly from the Odoo platform. This feature enhances customer engagement by enabling users to view and download comprehensive catalogues, improving the shopping experience.tor, streamlining and expediting the calculation process for POS employees.

Product Catalog Generation:
